# Profitability-Analysis-KPI-Dashboard-Power-BI
This project is an independent data case study for a boutique hospitality unit (Bikta BaZait) focused on improving business profitability through data-driven pricing optimization and guest-retention incentives.
The project includes data modeling, cost allocation, KPI design, profitability analysis, and post-implementation performance comparison (Before vs After).

Business Objective<br>
Identify operational inefficiencies and low-profit periods, improve pricing and cost structure, and evaluate the financial impact of implemented business actions.<br>

Key Insights<br>
Weekend stays were previously low-profit / negative under the old pricing model<br>
Introducing an added-value weekend package improved revenue quality<br>
Return-guest incentives increased booking consistency and profit per stay<br>
Post-implementation period showed +13% improvement in net profit<br>

Tools & Methods<br>
Power BI - Data modeling, DAX measures, KPI dashboard<br>
CSV-based synthetic dataset (Bookings, Costs, Calendar)<br>
Profitability & contribution margin analysis<br>
Before/After (Post-Implementation) performance tracking<br>
